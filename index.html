01 What are property rights in Pakistan?

Article 172 states that: 'Every citizen shall have the right to acquire, hold and dispose of property in any part of Pakistan, subject to the Constitution and any reasonable restrictions imposed by law in the public interest'.

02 How property is transferred in Pakistan?

According to the transfer of property act, three scenarios allow you to transfer property ownership in Pakistan. Inheritance – Transfer of property ownership after someone's death. Gift – Property transferred to someone as a gift. Buying or selling – Transfer of ownership under the context of buying property.

03 Can foreigners own property in Pakistan?
